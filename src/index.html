TRIBUTE TO A SUPER S-HERO.

And Heaven gains an angel...

The late Prof. Dora Nkem Akunyili (July 1954 - 2004) was the Director General of National Agency For Food And Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC).

An embodiment of wisdom, charisma, eloquence, intelligence, filled with so much compassion for her country, a bundle of energy.

Late Prof. Dora Akunyili stood for the right things till her dying days one woman who stood at the frontline to combat against fake drugs, illicit consumption of drugs,not one to twist the truth to soothe your fragile ego, she was a huge pin in the neck of malicious and dubious folks in the country.

She was never afraid to confront the guilty ones irrespective of personal affiliations, one who was dogged, fearless, bold, and audaciously brutal with the TRUTH! If being fearless, audacious, bold, resilient were a face, Dora Akunyili wore the look to perfection.

A hero is someone who has given his or herself to something bigger than oneself. "Joseph Campbell"

It is often said heroes never die, they only live on forever in the hearts and minds of those who follow in their steps.

And to that woman whose legacy lives on Dora Akunyili...you are my (S)HERO!
